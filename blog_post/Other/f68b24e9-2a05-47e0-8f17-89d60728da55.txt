Nearly 20 years after a deadly shooting in Riverside County, authorities have made an arrest in their decades-old cold case investigation.

This incident highlights the ongoing challenges of solving cold cases using traditional investigative methods. However, advances in digital forensics and genealogy research have transformed the way detectives approach these types of cases.

Cold case investigators use advanced technologies like DNA analysis and social media tracking to identify potential suspects.
