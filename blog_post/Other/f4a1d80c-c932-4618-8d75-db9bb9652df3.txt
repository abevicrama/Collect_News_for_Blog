If you're looking for a gripping subversive cosmic horror novella that explores themes of identity, power dynamics, and social justice, then The Ballad of Black Tom is the book for you. This 149-page behemoth may seem daunting, but trust us, it's worth devoting your time to. With its complex magic system and nuanced characterization, this novel has a lot to offer readers looking for something more than just another typical superhero story. As we delve into the world of Black Tom, you'll find yourself fully invested in his journey - despite not being a traditional hero. To get started, we highly recommend picking up a physical copy or supporting your local library.
