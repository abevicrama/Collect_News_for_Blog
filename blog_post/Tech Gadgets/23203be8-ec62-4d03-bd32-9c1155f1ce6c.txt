The new year brings a fresh wave of concerns about home security as burglars become increasingly sophisticated in their methods. To protect your property from potential intruders, it's essential to stay ahead of the curve when it comes to security systems.

One often-overlooked aspect of home security is signage, which can be easily exploited by determined trespassers. Lights that indicate a camera is active can be seen for miles and may alert burglars to your presence. Moreover, some signs specifically claim to show if you have a security system in place, potentially giving intruders valuable information about your security measures.

To safeguard your home and its contents, consider investing in smart locks with advanced sensors that can monitor entry points and ensure they're always locked at the right times. This is especially crucial when using DIY monitoring services like ADT or Vivint Home Security.

By incorporating these technical solutions into your home security system, you'll significantly improve protection against potential burglars.
